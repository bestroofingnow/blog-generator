## üí° Pro Tips for Best Results

### For High Rankings (SEO)
- Use specific, long-tail topics: "Landscape Lighting for Historic Homes in Mooresville"
- Generate neighborhood guides (more searchable)
- Create 5-6 sections for comprehensive coverage
- Include variations of keywords in H2s

### For Higher Conversions
- Use "Luxury and premium" tone for high-end neighborhoods
- Include more "before/after" language
- Focus on lifestyle benefits alongside practical
- Generate 5 sections minimum for authority

### For Social Media Virality
- Use "Casual and conversational" tone
- Generate "Inspirational and lifestyle" for aspirational content
- Keep 4-5 sections (easier to break into bite-sized posts)
- Focus on emotional hooks

### For Thought Leadership
- Use "Educational and informative" tone
- Generate "Expert Tips" blog type
- Aim for 6-7 sections for depth
- Focus on specific, actionable advice
